Two floors · 24,000 sq ft

Indoor and air-conditioned. 12,000 sq ft upstairs alone.

A big indoor flea market on Hobson Ave — two floors of antiques, vintage, and the unexpected. Hundreds of booths from local vendors. New finds every week.

Six specialties under one roof

What you'll find

  • 01

    Antiques

    Grandfather clocks, leather horses, model ships, the rare and the wild.

  • 02

    Collectibles

    Pokémon, Magic, comics, vinyl, action figures. The hunt is the fun.

  • 03

    WW1 & WW2 Memorabilia

    A specialty corner. Genuine medals, uniforms, and field gear — regularly rotated. We buy, too.

  • 04

    Mid-Century

    Lamps, lounge chairs, the warm-wood era. Curated rooms, not a pile.

  • 05

    Furniture

    From farmhouse to formal. Real wood, real prices, real character.

  • 06

    Coins

    Pennies, nickels, dimes, mint sets. A serious case for the serious collector.
